About the Project

The purpose of this project is to provide its users with a tool through which they can stay constantly updated about the status of coronavirus patients specifically in their community or network, and also to warn them against the same as soon as there is an instance of a case that could travel through their network and ultimately reach them and also tell the degree of vulnerability they currently face.


Idea Behind the Project

The idea here is that our social relations can actually be considered just as a network or a graph which would give a much more mathematical and scientific approach towards the problem. Next the web tool will organize its users into a social network, according to certain information that will be collected from them. And based upon that, certain algorithm will be implemented on this data and the data from the government regarding the positive cases of the disease to tell the users how vulnerable they currently are, what is the level of penetration of the disease in their network, specifically; and also advise them to socially distance from the people they know who may turn out to be potential carriers of the virus.
